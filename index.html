Who is Behind the Lens?

Jen Reyes is a water photographer that specializes in coastal/beach, sunset, surf, and wave photography.

Viewing the world through a camera lens, Jen has learned that every photo has a story, and the perfect photo does in fact speak a thousand words. However, each photo can be seen through multiple interpretations depending on the individual. People have the choice to evaluate deeper and participate on subjects that interest them, and even ones they have yet to discover.

In the early 2000s, Jen developed a surprising passion for photography after attending many music shows to support local and national bands / artists in San Diego. She pursued a career as a photographer specializing in live music photography with local San Diego companies, Eventvibe.com and Bandvibe.com.

Around 2014, Jen wanted to take her photography career a step further mixing her love of the ocean, beaches and surfing. Fusing her passion and active beach lifestyle, Jen captures photoJENic moments of many kinds of people, coastal places she has traveled, and water action sports through her lens.
